Pepperoni!!

This is the most popular pizza topping in the US, prized for its salty, savory, smoky, and slightly spicy flavor, as well as the chewy-crispy texture is develops when cooked.

Pepperoni is a variety of spicy, cured salami made from pork and beef, seasoned with paprika and chili peppers.

"Cup and char" pepperoni is traditional pepperoni with a natural casing that will curl up into small, crispy "cups" when cooked at high heat
