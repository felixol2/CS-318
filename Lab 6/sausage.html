Sausage!!

Italian sausage is the most popular and traditional sausage on pizza

Many other types of sausage can be used from spicy chorizo to smoky andouille

Chorizo is a Spanish sausage, seasoned with paprika, adds a smoky spicy flavor that works well with stronger toppings like onions and peppers

FRESH SAUSAGE SHOULD ALWAYS BE PRE-COOKED AND DRAINED TO PREVENT THE CRUST FROM BECOMING SOGGY
